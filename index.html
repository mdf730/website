Hello, World

Matthew Fabian is a rising senior at the University of Notre Dame. He expects to graduate in May 2018 with a double major in Computer Science and Computer Engineering and a minor in Engineering Corporate Practice.

His research experience includes four semesters in Professor Sauer’s Power Systems lab. He spent the past two summers interning with the Naval Nuclear Laboratory, most recently working with the Advanced Development group for Instrumentation and Control hardware/software.

In his free time, Matthew enjoys ringing with the Notre Dame Handbell Choir.
